About Next Inn

International with a local twist

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Next Inn was born from a passion for combining international luxury standards with authentic local experiences. Our beachside penthouse is designed to offer guests an unparalleled stay with breathtaking ocean views, premium amenities, and personalized service.

Luxury Redefined

Every detail is carefully curated to provide the ultimate in comfort and elegance, from premium furnishings to world-class amenities.

Personalized Service

Our dedicated team ensures every guest receives personalized attention, creating memorable experiences tailored to your preferences.

Stunning Location

Nestled by the beach, our penthouse offers panoramic ocean views and easy access to pristine shores and local attractions.

Our Philosophy

At Next Inn, we believe luxury is in the details. From the carefully selected furnishings to the locally-sourced amenities, every aspect of your stay is thoughtfully curated to provide comfort, style, and a true sense of place.

We combine international standards of excellence with authentic local touches, creating a unique experience that celebrates both global sophistication and regional charm.

The Next Inn Experience

Our 5-story penthouse offers a unique accommodation experience with each floor featuring distinct room types and amenities. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, we have the perfect space for your needs.
